Smart JSS Image Compliance Detection - Label Rules

1. is_female
2. has_jio_jacket
3. has_laminated_jio_promotional_paper

- clear visibility of torso/jacket area
- paper visible if present
- no blur

- no group photo

Operational principle:
- model prediction must include confidence
- low-confidence or unclear cases should be flagged for manual review
